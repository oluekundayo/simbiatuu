About The Founder

Simbiat Atinuke Awe

I am a working professional, a single mother, and a woman who followed the traditional path — education, employment, responsibility — yet reached a point where I realized that a stable job alone was not a long-term financial strategy.

Like many professionals, I worked hard, showed up consistently, and did everything that was expected — yet I began to see the limitations of relying solely on a salary for freedom, flexibility, and future security. I wanted more than survival. I wanted options.

My turning point came when I was introduced to a structured global mentorship and business education system. Through this system, I experienced a shift not just financially, but mentally and emotionally.

"I created SMG to introduce serious professionals to this structured mentorship pathway and guide them through the process with transparency, support, and integrity."

SMG exists for professionals who know they are capable of more — and are ready to explore a smarter, more sustainable way to build financial independence.
